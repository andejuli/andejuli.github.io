Target Audience:

Who: Any person wanting to get a quick look at the weather for their Idaho hometown.

Age: 12 to 100

Technology: Users will have access to the internet and devices with browsers that will run the web application.

Personas:

Occupation: Homemaker

Motivation to use the site: Has kids who wait at a bus stop.

Occupation: Typical Idaho company office worker.

Commutes and weather is important to him.

Scenarios:

  1. What is the weather like today?
  2. What is a quick and easy way to get the weather?
  3. How can I get just the weather for my hometown quickly?
  4. How can I get weather information that I can understand and is readable?
  5. I need weather from a reliable, up-to-date source.
